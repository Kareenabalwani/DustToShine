F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take to complete a simple wash?

    It is entirely dependent on the amount of your order. Simple wash takes on average 1-2 business days to complete.

  • Is there any possibility of fabric damage during the stain removal service due to the use of scrub or chemicals?

    Stains are pre-treated with the appropriate type and amount of detergent. We do not scrub the stains too hard. We also select the appropriate wash cycle and temperature based on the fabric. We never use harmful chemicals in any of our services.

  • Is it possible to cancel the requested service?

    Yes, you can. However, the service must be in the picked-up state. You cannot cancel the service once it has reached the processing stage.

  • Is there an extra charge for delivery?

    No. Our service includes both pickup and delivery. However, there are additional fees for the urgent delivery option.

  • Can you remove ink stains from my white shirt?

    Knowing what type of ink caused the damage is essential for ink stain removal. It also depends on the fabric that is affected.

  • what exactly does your shoe cleaning service entail?

    Our Shoe Cleaning service includes a thorough cleaning of the entire shoe. Everything is cleaned, from the insole to the stitching, and each shoe is thoroughly cleaned to look as close to new as possible.
